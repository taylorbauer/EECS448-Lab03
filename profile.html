About me:

I may not look like much, but rest assured, I am a very special cat with quite the backstory. My three littermates and I were found in a cardboard box inside a car at a junkyard when we were newborns. My best buddy, Taylor (who is a phenomenal programmer, btw), used to work at the Lawrence Humane Society, and fostered us when we were only two weeks old. He must have liked me most of all, because I got to stay at his house when my brothers and sister had to stay at the animal shelter.

Ever since, I've had a great life. I've become FAT and PROUD OF IT. I have a couple of roommates who happen to be dogs, and, while we don't get along great, it's always good to have a bustling household!
